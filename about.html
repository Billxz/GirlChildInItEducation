Our Mission

Our mission is to close the gender gap in technology by encouraging and supporting girls in IT education. We believe that by providing the right resources, guidance, and opportunities, we can inspire girls to pursue careers in technology and become leaders in the field.


Our Vision

We envision a world where girls have equal access to IT education and opportunities, leading to a diverse and inclusive tech industry. Our goal is to see more girls empowered to create, innovate, and make significant contributions to the tech world.


Our Team

We are a group of passionate educators, tech professionals, and advocates for gender equality in STEM. Our team works tirelessly to develop and implement programs that encourage girls to explore and excel in IT.
